About

Thanks for looking at my website, my name is Liam Scott a web/app developer from Essex, England.

I have held many different roles within multiple careers. My first role began when I was 17 and I joined the British Army where I had a successful career specialising in telecommunications within the Royal Artillery.

After leaving the Army in 2015 I moved to Australia for 2 years and gained a lot more confidence in myself while working for a sales and marketing company. Face to face sales gained me experience in setting KPI's and hitting targets both individually and as a team.

Upon returning to England I tried numerous jobs before finding the right time to start coding, which then took me down the path of a web developer with HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript. Then when I was ready I took and passed the Mircosoft 70-480 exam.

Since passing the exam I have compeleted two very different projects Gazetteer and Company Directory, both of which were a lot of fun to put together and rewarding at the end when you see it all come together.
